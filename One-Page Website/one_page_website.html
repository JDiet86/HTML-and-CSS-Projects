Sea Turtles: An endangered species

"Over the last 200 years, human activities have tipped
the scales against the survival of these ancient mariners.
Slaughtered for their eggs, meat, skin, and shells, sea
turtles suffer from poaching and over-exploitation.
They also face habitat destruction and accidental
capture—known as bycatch—in fishing gear."
